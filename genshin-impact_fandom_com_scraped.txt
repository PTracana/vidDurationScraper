Quest Types
Story Quests
are
Quests
revolving around
Playable Characters
and their interactions with the
Traveler
. Character
Story Quest Chapters
are named after the character's
constellation
,
Hangout Events
are the character name, and
Tribal Chronicles
are the tribe name.
Most Story Quests give the player the opportunity to trial the featured character for a brief period. This includes cases where said character may be in the player's active party, in which case they will be temporarily replaced with a trial version.
A few character Story Quests are required in order to progress further into the
Archon Quest
series. Other character Story Quests are required to access some
Weekly Bosses
.
Some Story Quests are part of
Events
, and may require specific character Story Quests and/or Archon Quests in order to progress. Since
Version 3.2
however, these event Story Quests no longer require character Story Quests, however the game recommends players complete said quests first for the best experience and avoiding in-game spoilers.

How to Unlock
[
]
All Story Quests have requirements that the player has to meet to unlock the quest. These requirements may differ between each act of the story chapter. Requirements include:
Reaching the required
Adventure Rank
.
Completing prior quests in the chapter.
Completing Archon Quest acts.
Completing other Characters' Story Quests.
Using the required number of Story Keys.
Story Keys
[
]
Main article:
Story Key
Story Key
Story Keys
are quest unlock items that players after Adventure Rank 26 can claim every 8
Commissions
completed (i.e., 2 days of 4 Commissions). Story Keys will not be earned automatically and must be claimed from the Story Quests menu once eligible. A total of 3 Story Keys can be held at one time, but the counter will still continue to max out at 8, so one is able to immediately be claimed after one has been spent.
Hangout Event Acts require 2 Story Keys and repeating the same Hangout will not require more.
Story Quest Acts do not require Story Keys (Since
Version 5.4
).
Tribal Chronicles Acts do not require Story Keys.

Trivia
[
]
The following characters have Story Quest Chapters whose names differ from the character's Constellation name, in order of introduction:
Zhongli
:
Historia Antiqua Chapter
and
Lapis Dei
Constellation.
Neuvillette
:
Diluvies Chapter
and
Leviathan Judicator
Constellation.
The following are the only Story Quests that are prerequisites to progress the main
Archon Quests
:
Kamisato Ayaka
:
The Whispers of the Crane and the White Rabbit
Razor
:
The Meaning of Lupical
Requires
Amber
:
Wind, Courage, and Wings
Yoimiya
:
Dreamlike Timelessness
The second Story Quest act of Characters will be accompanied by the introduction of a new
Weekly Boss
, with the exception of:
Yoimiya: Star-Pickers' Passage
Cyno: Oathkeeper
The following characters do not appear as a
Trial Character
in their Story Quest:
Albedo
:
Traveler Observation Report
Neuvillette
:
The Remains of the Past Day
Furina
:
"The Little Oceanid"
Arlecchino
:
When the Hearth-Flame Goes Out
Yoimiya
was the first non-
Archon
character to obtain a second Story Quest act.
Kaeya
was the first character to have both a Story Quest and Hangout Event.
Story Quests for 4-star characters have not been added since
Version 1.0
. Similarly, no Hangout Events for 5-star characters have ever been added since their debut in
Version 1.4
.
